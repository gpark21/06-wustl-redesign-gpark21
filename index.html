About

The Sam Fox School is currently comprised of five buildings, located on the eastern portion of the Danforth Campus, as well as one facility located off campus.

Programs

The Sam Fox School offers a vast array of programs in art, architecture, and design. In addition to programs for undergraduate and graduate students both on campus in St. Louis and internationally, the School offers summer programs for everyone from high school students to professionals, and is involved in a variety of initiatives with partners across campus and throughout the St. Louis area.

Community

The Sam Fox School is, above all else, a vital, world-changing community of artists, architects, designers, curators, and scholars. The people here are always at the heart of what we're doing, from our talented and creative students, to our nationally and internationally distinguished faculty, to our passionate and hard-working staff, to the alumni who leave the School and change the world through their work in a diverse array of fields.
